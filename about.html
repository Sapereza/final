The Perfect Destination

Taking You Beyond Extraordinary

Exotic Travel is a full-service luxury travel agency located in Boston, Massachusetts.
Founded in 1994 by Bostonian entrepreneur, Sarah Perez-Anderson, our professional team of over 100
travel experts has planned more than 500,000 vacations.


As a preferred partner of the world’s foremost travel brands and a proud member of the prestigious Signature Travel Network, we’ve built trusted relationships with hundreds of travel companies, destination specialists, and local experts worldwide so you always get the best vacation value available – savings, service and support and you won’t find anywhere else.


With seven Boston locations plus a robust online division, we are a full service agency that specializes in customized vacation planning for all types of travel. The best part – there’s no obligation, no pressure, and with our price-match promise, you never pay more for your vacation, only get more.
